Help

This is the help page for the website. If you have any questions or need help with anything, feel free to ask.

FAQ

  • Q: What is this website?

    This website is a platform for users to share and discover code.

  • Q: How do I create an account?

    Click on the "Sign Up" link in the top right corner of the page and follow the instructions.

  • Q: How do I upload a file?

    Click on the "Plus" button in the same level as the "Library" text.

  • Q: How do I search for a file?

    Use the search bar in the top right corner of the page.

  • Q: How do I report a problem?

    Click on the "Contact" link in the footer and header and follow the instructions.
